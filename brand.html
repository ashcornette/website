owduv

brand identity · household restocking

owduv tracks the everyday things a home runs through — soap, detergent, coffee — and nudges you before you run out, not after. the identity keeps that calm and plainspoken: lowercase, unhurried, friendly without trying too hard.

the mark

the logo lives in the "o" — a container filling up, drawn in the green that means stocked. one glyph holds the whole idea: a thing, and how full it is. it survives the squeeze too — legible at favicon size, intact in a single colour, at home on light, dark, or a solid green tile.

palette

green leads and carries the stock states — full, low, out. cobalt is the accent and the predictive cues. the neutrals run a touch warm-green so the whole thing reads fresh rather than clinical — and it all has a dark mode.

voice

never out of the everyday.

owduv speaks in lowercase and short sentences — a note on the fridge, not a sales pitch. it talks in outcomes ("you're stocked", "you're running low"), never about the tracking underneath.
