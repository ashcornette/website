draft — week 5 · capture flow · phase 2 research

The capture loop

the hard part was never the alert — it's the logging. so the app doesn't ask you to log. it reads the purchase you already made, learns how fast you get through things, and only asks for a tap when it needs to correct a guess.

How signals get in

most of the signal enters at zero touch — receipts and orders. scan and manual are only there to catch what receipts miss. everything between intake and the nudge happens on the phone: raw purchase data is reduced to date, sku and quantity, and never leaves it.

The catch — to stress-test in research
cash and un-receipted buys are invisible. variable pack sizes wobble the cadence. and genuinely irregular items — bleach, batteries — don't follow a curve. those are the edges the interviews need to probe.
