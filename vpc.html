  • Household members unaligned on what's needed
  • Only notice the problem when it's too late
Gain creators
How you create customer gains
  • Zero mental load — app tracks patterns automatically
  • Smarter restocking than Subscribe & Save
  • One place for the whole household
  • Direct reorder links save time at peak intent
  • Free tier lowers barrier to first value
Gains
Outcomes and benefits they want
  • Never run out of essentials unexpectedly
  • Less mental load around household management
  • Shared visibility across the household
  • Feel organized without extra effort
  • Save time on shopping trips
fit achieved when pain relievers + gain creators directly address customer jobs, pains, and gains
