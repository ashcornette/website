wireframes

low- to mid-fidelity — structure and hierarchy over polish. status reads two ways at once: a fill level (full / half / empty) that echoes the owduv mark, and the brand's green / amber / red. five screens cover the core of the app — home, item detail, reorder, the shared household, and first-run.

the screen owduv opens to. it leads with what needs attention, shows a predicted run-out for every item, and keeps reorder out of the way until something is actually low.
